The Stone House

A contemporary residence celebrating the raw beauty of local stone, with minimalist design and organic integration with the landscape.


The Stone House is a contemporary residential project that places raw, locally-sourced stone at the heart of its design language. The architecture responds to its natural surroundings while providing modern comfort and spatial flow.

Project Management

End-to-end project management including contractor coordination, procurement, and on-site delivery.

Key Features

  • Locally sourced stone exterior and interiors
  • Thermal mass stone walls for climate control
  • Minimalist interior design
  • Large glazed openings with landscape views
  • Hand-finished plaster walls
  • Organic site integration

Architecture

The architectural language is rooted in the raw materiality of locally-sourced stone. The building mass is sculpted to respond to the landscape, creating sheltered courtyards and framed views of the surrounding terrain.
